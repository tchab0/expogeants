His Story

The Giant Smuggler is less harmless. He grew diamonds without authorisation, selling them on the black market. The intensive use of plastic on Oniris has left a thin layer of it on the soil in many regions, including where he grew his crops. As a result, diamonds now grow very badly, sometimes too big and too simple, but more often small and fuzzy. For him too, times are hard and have made him suspicious. And a little angry.
