His Story

The giant walker was returning from a lovely stroll by the river. As always, for the past 33 months now, she had put on the mandatory equipment (Standard E783) to go out near aquatic environments. Thankfully! She was attacked by a Mustanta-Muska, a species capable of projecting up to two and a half liters of acids of all kinds (whatever they find in rivers). Still, she managed to pick some flowers and was proud to show them to me! I’m not very skilled in botany, so I couldn’t figure out what they were.
