His Story

Climate change sometimes brings torrential rain. This was the case in the Milenjara-Sekeco region. I had the great good fortune to arrive in this totally disaster-stricken region during an eight-week rainstorm that could scarcely have been imagined on Earth before 2035 2023! In the rush I photographed the urbmejstro of the first town I found. His communications advisers had begged him to go and meet his constituents to show them a little compassion. I bumped into him on the way back (I think he took me for a malgranda administrita ). The photo shown here was taken at the very beginning of a flood that drowned the poor creature just after it had had time to save me. Perhaps I took too long to take the photo, which also caused me to lose a flash that was very important to me.
