His Story

The giants also have incredible amusement parks where you can freeze to simulate life in the old days, when the temperature rarely rose above 30°, so that you can cover yourself in the beautiful coats of yesteryear. But the system, which has to increase its power regularly, often gets out of control and the giants find themselves trapped in the ice. This happened at a time when I was there to relax and enjoy a bit of fresh air and, let's face it, life. I happened to bump into the main inspector, who had come to investigate the cause of the new breakdown. It was so cold that day that even he was practically frozen, it was like he was petrified ! Fortunately, the system makes enough money that the unfortunates who freeze for good are not enough to call into question its operation.
