His Story

The virtual disappearance of water on Oniris has turned the quest for the Graalo into a struggle for survival. During my stay on Oniris a rumour spread: the knight Lanceloto had finally found the Graalo! I spent a fortune on a private flight to meet him for a few minutes at the other end of the planet. And I arrived just in time, on a beautiful starry night, to see him preparing to drink the fragile chalice: 18 ml of rainwater, the last shower to fall on Oniris years before and miraculously preserved. I never knew if he was trying to impress me or if he was just fighting a bit to get to the Graalo. The Giants, too powerful for their planet, got into the habit of fighting each other. Before the great upheavals, they had no other adversaries to distract them.
