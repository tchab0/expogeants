His Story

To reassure the population, the leaders of the giants have chosen to use illusionists. They divert the attention of the unfortunate inhabitants of Oniris and keep them dreaming until the Collapse. Illusionists, along with entertainers, are now the most pampered and best paid creatures on Oniris. They deserve it, it's a really useful profession!
This one is one of the best: during the great fires in the Pluvegiona region, he managed to make butterflies (butterflies have long since disappeared on Oniris) appear from over 76 gradocelsia in the shade of his survival blanket! It's marvellous, he's brilliant!
