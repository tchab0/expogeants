Her Story

Or ‘Petite danseuse à la nature (bien) morte’ (Little dancer with a (very) dead nature) In certain regions of Oniris, the tap water is completely polluted and for some years now has contained more petroleum residues than water. The authorised showers, still one a week, do not help matters and the iridescence of the skin is becoming difficult to hide. The little dancer is not to be pitied: plastics have been banned for years on Oniris and it's clear that her social class allows her to enjoy them to the full. And if she looks so fit despite her young age, it's because she's been smuggling not diamonds, but bottled water!
